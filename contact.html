Room Rates

We charge by occupancy, including children/babies. We've found that more people in a room require more work for cleaning, laundry, etc. and thus have to charge for the extra expenses. Our rates start at $79 and increase based on number of occupants and time of year.

Penthouse Rates

The base rate is up to 6 guests (including children), and maximum occupancy is 8 people, with a 3-night minimum throughout the year, except for holidays (Christmas, New Years, Easter) when we have a 4-night minimum.

To secure your reservation,we require a 50% non-refundable deposit due at time of booking, with the balance due 30 days prior to arrival, plus a $250 security/cleaning deposit.
